# financial-cyber-security-strategy
A strategic cybersecurity consultancy report for Global Finance Inc. assessing the threat landscape, CTI implementation, and NIST/ISO framework alignment
Financial Sector Cyber Security Strategy: Global Finance Inc.

📄 Project Overview: This repository contains a comprehensive cybersecurity consultancy report developed as part of a University module. The research focuses on assessing the cybersecurity stance of Global Finance Inc., analyzing the modern threat landscape, and formulating a strategic roadmap for financial sector resilience.

🛡️ Strategic Analysis: 
The research evaluates the strategic implementation of Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) to move Global Finance Inc. toward a proactive defense posture. Key focuses include:Framework Alignment: Integration of ISO/IEC 27001 for risk-based management and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework for technical agility.

Resilience Measures: Implementation of Zero-Trust networks, Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), and staff awareness training.

👥 Threat Actor Profiling:
A deep-dive into the diverse motives and tactics of actors targeting financial institutions, including:Nation-States: Government-backed espionage and infrastructure disruption.Insider Threats: Malicious or negligent misuse of privileged access by employees or contractors.Cyber Criminals: Profit-driven campaigns focused on data theft and ransomware.

🔍Vulnerability Case Study: 
EternalBlue (CVE-2017-0144)The report features a chronological analysis of the EternalBlue exploit, from its development by the NSA to its eventual leak by the "Shadow Brokers".Impact: Evaluation of the WannaCry attack, which leveraged this exploit to cause an estimated $4 billion in global losses.Technical Analysis: Exploitation of the SMBv1 protocol to facilitate lateral movement and ransomware deployment.

📊 Industry Trends & Data Analysis:
Analysis of the Verizon 2025 Data Breach Investigations Report shows that system intrusion accounts for 53% of attack types in the financial sector. The research highlights a positive correlation between the rise of sophisticated exploits and the escalating costs of ransomware recovery.

🛠️ Access Control Mechanisms:
Comparative research into modern access control models to ensure 'least privilege' across the enterprise:

RBAC (Role-Based Access Control): Streamlining access based on organizational roles.

ABAC (Attribute-Based Access Control): Flexible, context-aware decisions based on user and system attributes.

Eugene Nleya authored this report as a digital solutions consultancy case study.
